This article consists of detailed guides to turn off specific tracking setups.
Currently, we provide instructions to turn off the following tracking methods on a per-destination basis: 

- [Facebook / Meta](#facebook-meta)
    - [The Shopify Facebook Sales Channel](#turning-off-the-shopify-facebook-sales-channel)

- [GA4](#GA4)

- [TikTok](#tiktok)

## Facebook / Meta 

#### Turning off the Shopify Facebook Sales Channel data sharing

1. Navigate to your <OutboundLink linkText="Shopify Admin Dashboard" linkTarget="https://admin.shopify.com/" />.

2. Click on <IconText name="angle-right" text="Sales channels" iconLocation = "after"/> and choose **Facebook & Instagram** from the *Installed sales channels* menu.

3. Click on **Settings**.

    <Frame>
        ![settings-facebook](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-facebook-sales-channel/shopify-facebook-sales-channel-0.png)
    </Frame>

4. Click on **Change** in the **Share data** submenu
    <Frame>
        ![settings-facebook-1](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-facebook-sales-channel/shopify-facebook-sales-channel-1.png)
    </Frame>

5. Turn the **Share data** switch **off** (it should be gray) and press **Save**.
    <Frame>
        ![settings-facebook-2](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-facebook-sales-channel/shopify-facebook-sales-channel-2.png)
    </Frame>

6. You are done.

## GA4

#### Turning off the Shopify Google Sales Channel data sharing

1. Navigate to your <OutboundLink linkText="Shopify Admin Dashboard" linkTarget="https://admin.shopify.com/" />.

2. Click on <IconText name="angle-right" text="Sales channels" iconLocation = "after"/> and choose **Google & YouTube** from the *Installed sales channels* menu.

3. Click on **Settings**.

    <Frame>
        ![settings-google-0](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-ga4-sales-channel/shopify-ga4-sales-channel-0.png)
    </Frame>

4. Click on **Disconnect** in the **Your Google Analytics property** submenu
    <Frame>
        ![settings-google-1](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-ga4-sales-channel/shopify-ga4-sales-channel-1.png)
    </Frame>

5. You are done.

## TikTok

#### Turning off the Shopify TikTok Sales Channel data sharing

1. Navigate to your <OutboundLink linkText="Shopify Admin Dashboard" linkTarget="https://admin.shopify.com/" />.

2. Click on <IconText name="angle-right" text="Sales channels" iconLocation = "after"/> and choose **TikTok** from the *Installed sales channels* menu.

3. Click on **Settings**, then **Data sharing** and finally click on **Disconnect**

    <Frame>
        ![settings-tiktok-1](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-tiktok-sales-channel/shopify-tiktok-sales-channel-1.png)
    </Frame>

4. After reading and confirming the impact of disconnecting the pixel, click on **Disconnect**.
    <Frame>
        ![settings-google-2](/images/guides/turn-off-tracking/shopify-tiktok-sales-channel/shopify-tiktok-sales-channel-2.png)
    </Frame>

5. You are done.
