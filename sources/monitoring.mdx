## Overview

Converge provides the following tools to monitor and debug whether your Sources are working correctly:
- [Source Logs](#source-logs)
- [Source Warnings](#source-warnings) 

---

## Source Logs

Converge keeps detailed logs of every event that gets sent to Converge including [event properties](/sources/converge-spec), [profile properties](/sources/source-concepts#profile-properties) and [aliases](/sources/source-concepts#aliases).

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="location-dot" title="Where to find Source Logs">
1. Navigate to the [Sources Tab](https://app.runconverge.com/sources) in your Converge dashboard
2. Click on the Source you want to debug
   ![pick a source](/images/sources/monitoring/source-monitoring-0.png)
3. Click on the **Logs** tab to see the logs
  ![pick logs](/images/sources/monitoring/source-monitoring-1.png)
4. Click on any of the Log Lines to inspect the **event properties**, **profile properties**, **aliases**, etc.
</Accordion>

<Accordion icon="filter" title="Filter through the logs">
- **By Event Name**: You can select a subset of events to show in the log by clicking on **Select events** and then ticking the relevant events.
- **By Date**: You can use the date picker in the right top corner to select log lines from a particular date range.
</Accordion>

<Accordion icon="download" title="Exporting logs">
You can export your log selection to CSV using the **Export to .csv** button in the right top corner. 
Log entries are limited to 10,000 lines.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Source Warnings

Converge will automatically flag when events are not conform the [Converge Event Spec](/sources/converge-spec) and give you pointers as to how to modify your events to be compliant with the spec.


<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="location-dot" title="Where to find Source Warnings">
1. Navigate to the [Sources Tab](https://app.runconverge.com/sources) in your Converge dashboard
2. Click on the Source you want to debug
   ![pick a source](/images/sources/monitoring/source-monitoring-0.png)
3. Click on the **Warnings** tab to see the appropriate warnings. Each warning includes information as to what `property` is malformed, as well as detailed log lines to inspect.
  ![see warning info](/images/sources/monitoring/source-monitoring-2.png)
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
