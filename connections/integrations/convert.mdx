## Installation Instructions

<Steps>
<Step icon="bullseye" title="1. Create a new revenue goal">

1. In your Convert dashboard, go to **Goals**  and select **New Goal**.
2. Click on **Revenue goal**.
    <Frame> ![revenue-goal-picker](/images/connections/convert/revenue-goal.png) </Frame>
3. Give your goal a name, e.g. `[Converge] Revenue` and choose **Manual Revenue Tracking**. You can leave Goal Description blank.
4. Click on **Save Goal**.
5. Copy the Revenue **Goal ID**, you will need it later.
    <Frame> ![revenue-goal-picker](/images/connections/convert/goal-id.png) </Frame>
6. Also write down your **Account Id**, you can find it in the top right corner of the Convert Dashboard.
    <Frame> ![account-id](/images/connections/convert/account-id.png) </Frame>
7. Lastly, navigate to **Configuration** > **General Settings** and copy your **Project ID**.
</Step>

<Step icon="list" title="2. Create the Converge Connection">
8. In Converge, click **Create a new connection** under the **Connections** tab. Select the **Convert** app.
9. Fill in the **Account ID**, **Project ID** and **Revenue Goal ID** you retrieved from the previous steps.
10. Click on **Create Connection**.

</Step>

</Steps>
