## Installation Instructions

<AccordionGroup >
<Accordion icon="list" title="Installation Instructions">

1. Navigate to your Microsoft Advertising Dashboards and choose **Tools > UET Tag**.

2. If you already have a UET Tag, copy the **Tag ID** and go to Step 6, otherwise go to Step 3
   &nbsp;

3. Click on **Create**
   &nbsp;

4. Choose a UET tag name, and UET Tag Description and click on **Save and next**.
   &nbsp;

5. Choose `Install the tag yourself` and click on **Next**. From the code snippet, copy your Tag ID (marked in pink in the screenshot below) and click on **Next**. After that click on **Done**
    <Frame>
    ![bing-1](/images/connections/bing-ads/bing-ads-connection-0.png)
    </Frame>

6. In your Converge dashboard, under Connections, click <IconText name="circle-plus" text="Create New Connection" iconLocation="before" iconType="regular"/ > and fill in your Tag ID. After that click **Create Connection**.
   &nbsp;

</Accordion>

<Accordion icon="circle-dollar-to-slot" title="Create your Conversion goals">

1. Navigate to your Microsoft Advertising Dashboards and choose **Tools > Conversion goals**.

2. Click on the **Create** button.
   &nbsp;

3. Choose `Website` as your conversion goal type and press **Next**.
   &nbsp;

4. Choose your conversion goal category and press **Next**.

<Info> For most businesses, we advise to set up at least a `Purchase` conversion goal but Converge supports any of the events in the [Bing Ads Event Mapping](#event-mapping).  </Info>
    
5. As a Goal Type, choose **Event** and click **Next**.
   &nbsp;

6. Give your conversion goal a name, e.g. `[Converge] Purchase` and choose `Conversion action value may vary` as **Revenue**. Leave the other options unchanged and click **Next**.
   <Frame>
   ![bing set up conversion action](/images/connections/bing-ads/bing-ads-conversion-0.png)
   </Frame>

7. Choose **Turn on enhanced conversions** and tick the checkbox to enable enhanced conversions. Click on **Save and next**.

8. Choose the Tag you set up in `Installation Instructions` and click **Skip base tag setup**.

9. Ignore the enhanced conversion instructions. Just click on **Next**.

10. Choose **Manual installation** and click **Next**.

11. Fill in `purchase` in the Action Name field.
     <Info> If you are not setting up a `purchase` event, you should check the [Bing Ads Event Mapping](#event-mapping) for the appropriate action name (e.g. `add_to_cart`)</Info>
   <Frame>
   ![bing install event tag](/images/connections/bing-ads/bing-ads-conversion-1.png)
   </Frame>
  
12. Choose **Track event on inline action** under **When do you want to track this event?**. Ignore the code generated below and click **Save and next.**

13. You are done. Your conversion goal should populate with conversions from the Bing Ads Connection.

<Note> Repeat the steps above for all the conversion goals you want to report on in Bing Ads </Note>
</Accordion>
<Warning> Once you have installed your Converge Connection, you should **pause or remove all existing Bing Ads tracking**. Leaving these on will lead to duplicate tracking.  </Warning>
</AccordionGroup>

## Event Mapping

Converge automatically maps the following events from the [Converge Event Spec](/sources/converge-spec) to the Bing Ads spec.

| Converge Event Name                                             | Bing Ads Event Name |
| --------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| [Viewed Product](/sources/converge-spec#viewed-product)         | `view_item`           |
| [Viewed Collection](/sources/converge-spec#viewed-collection)   | `view_item_list`      |
| [Added To Cart](/sources/converge-spec#added-to-cart)           | `add_to_cart`         |
| [Started Checkout](/sources/converge-spec#started-checkout)     | `begin_checkout`      |
| [Added Payment Info](/sources/converge-spec#added-payment-info) | `add_payment_info`    |
| [Placed Order](/sources/converge-spec#placed-order)             | `purchase`            |
