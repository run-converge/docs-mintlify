## Installation Instructions

<AccordionGroup >
<Accordion icon="list" title="Installation Instructions">

1. Navigate to your Microsoft Advertising Dashboards and choose **Tools > UET Tag**.

2. If you already have a UET Tag, copy the **Tag ID** and go to Step 6, otherwise go to Step 3
   &nbsp;

3. Click on **Create**
   &nbsp;

4. Choose a UET tag name, and UET Tag Description and click on **Save and next**.
   &nbsp;

5. Choose `Install the tag yourself` and click on **Next**. From the code snippet, copy your Tag ID (marked in pink in the screenshot below) and click on **Next**. After that click on **Done**
    ![bing-1](/images/connections/bing-ads/bing-ads-connection-0.png)

6. In your Converge dashboard, under Connections, click **Create New Connection** and fill in your Tag ID. After that click **Create Connection**.
   &nbsp;

</Accordion>
<Warning> Once you have installed your Converge Connection, you should **pause or remove all existing Bing Ads tracking**. Leaving these on will lead to duplicate tracking.  </Warning>
</AccordionGroup>

## Integration details
- Includes support for [Bing Enhanced Conversions](https://help.ads.microsoft.com/apex/index/3/en/60178)
- Includes support for the [Bing Dynamic Remarketing](https://about.ads.microsoft.com/en-gb/solutions/audience-targeting/dynamic-remarketing)
