## Installation Instructions

<AccordionGroup >
<Accordion icon="list" title="Installation Instructions">

1. Navigate to your [TikTok Events Manager](https://ads.tiktok.com/i18n/events_manager/). If the direct link does not open your Events Manager, you can acces it from the TikTok Ads Manager dashboard under **Tools** > **Events**.

2. From your Events Manager, **click on "Manage"** under **Web Events**.

3. Click on your pixel, you might have to go through 2FA.

4. From the TikTok Pixel menu, copy your **Pixel ID**, you will need it later. The Pixel ID looks as follows: `CJQBODJC77UB4MRTGFRF`

5. Navigate to the **Settings** subtab and scroll down until you find the **Generate Access Token**-button. Click on it to generate your token and keep it around for next steps.

6. In your Converge dashboard, under Connections, click **Create New Connection**.

7. Give your connection an approppriate name (e.g. `TikTok Connection | {YOUR_PIXEL_ID}`) and **choose the TikTok App**.

8. Paste in the Pixel ID you acquired in the earlier step in the **Pixel ID** field. Paste in the access token you acquired earlier in the **Access Token** field.

9. Fill in your homepage domain name in the **Your Domain name** field, it does not need to include `https://`.

10. Click on **Create Connection**
</Accordion>
<Warning> Once you have installed your Converge Connection, you should **pause or remove all existing TikTok tracking**. Leaving these on will lead to duplicate tracking.  </Warning>
